Contract Manufacturing

Bhavika Plastek Pvt Ltd is a contract manufacturer with a core competency in plastic injection molding. We partner with original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) on mechanical and electronic sub-assemblies to complete final assemblies and products.

Our OEM partners consider Bhavika Plastek Pvt Ltd contract manufacturing an extension of their manufacturing operations – providing them an integrated, streamlined, turnkey, cost effective manufacturing process for assemblies through finished products.

With our global locations, sourcing, resources and lean manufacturing systems, we are able to source and purchase needed materials and parts, mold and assemble the plastic components, and provide the finished assembly or product – packaged and shipped - saving you time and money!

OEMs partnering with Bhavika Plastek Pvt Ltd for contract manufacturing indicate the partnership has enabled them to free up resources to focus more on strategy, sales and core competencies and expand their opportunities, while at the same time realizing savings on facility, equipment, maintenance, labor, training, tooling, energy, material and overall manufacturing cost – resulting in a significant increase to their bottom line!

Infrastructure

Bhavika Plastek Pvt Ltd and NanoPolymers are equipped with latest ultra modern machines imported and procured from acclaimed manufacturers. The company believes in customer satisfaction and capturing the market share in plastic furniture as its motto with a vision provide high quality product at competitive prices.

Our global plastic injection molding systems offers original equipment manufacturers the advantages of developing projects locally in the India and then sourcing and manufacturing them in any one, or more, of our globally located facilities.

Each of our facilities are equipped with plastic injection molding machines and systems, and each of our molds designed and built, such that molds can run at any of our facilities – enabling our customers to benefit from a globally focused and equipped plastic injection molding system providing maximum flexibility and the best cost savings and competitive advantage!
