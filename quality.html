Bhavika Plastek Private Limited (BPPL) started its business operations in the year 2005 in Mallapur, Hyderabad. Since then, it has always been a stable inclined journey. With over 20+ years of experience in plastic moulds and polymers along with addressing varied range of customer needs, we stand as one of the finest superior manufacturers in both the neighbouring states AP & Telangana.

Nano Polymers India Pvt Ltd (NANO) started its commercial operations in the year 2011 in Gundlapally Industrial estate, Ongole. The main reason for this establishment was to cater to the unending demands from A.P state and its neighbourhoods. This firm not only serves the production of plastic furnitures but has also been steady in adding diversified range products like evaporative air coolers and washers.
